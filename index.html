Executive Summary

Nurse Navigators Pro is a comprehensive healthcare coordination and in-home clinical services firm serving UHNW families, senior living facilities, and healthcare networks across Arizona. Our six Strategic Business Units (SBUs) generate 125+ services spanning personal care, clinical governance, disease management, B2B consulting, technology, and professional education.

Business Model: Hybrid revenue combining hourly service delivery ($29–$65/hour), retainer-based governance ($1,500–$12,000/month), one-time assessments ($450–$5,000), and subscription services ($29/month).

Market Opportunity

  • Private Clients: UHNW families seeking "Absolute Assurance".
  • Healthcare Systems: Readmission prevention & discharge coordination.
  • Facilities: Regulatory compliance & interim leadership ($4.5k+).

Competitive Advantage

  • Trademarked protocols (Meridian Survey™, Stella Tier™).
  • Integrated B2B + B2C model reduces churn.
  • Clinical governance prevents costly hospitalizations.
